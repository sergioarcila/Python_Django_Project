Scouting Report

Addison Potts is a 2022 Combo Guard from Ft. Myers, FL and plays for Impact Basketball Academy in Sarasota, FL. She is a skilled combo-guard that elevates and delivers consistently on mid-range jumper and can knock down the 3. She has a smooth release point with confidence and can get to the rim and finish. Addison is a top 100 prospect Nationally and ranked as high as 15 in one major media in her 2022 class. FABC 5A 1st team All-State in 2018 and Player of The Year Finalist Lee County 2018. Addison verbally committed to Missouri in the 8th grade over other schools/offers that included: USF, Iowa State, Kansas, Illinois, FGCU, and Colorado. If you have any questions about Addison, please contact Andy Potts at (239) 823-3508 or email Andy at andy.potts@nliscouting.com.
